# Bio

Name: Colby Schulz

## Early Life & Childhood

- Grew up in Coal Valley, Illinois, a small town of approximately 2,000 people.
- Childhood activities: soccer, inline speed skating (competitive team), boys choir.
- Holds a black belt in Tae Kwon Do.

## Middle School

- Began listening to bands like Deftones, Metallica, and Blink-182.
- Started playing guitar.

## High School

- Ran cross country and track all four years.
- Competed at the Illinois state championships all four years of high school.
- Conference champion in the 800m dash with a time of 1:59.
- Worked at a pizza place.
- Held a job detasseling corn.
- Had an early interest in engineering; completed an internship at John Deere while still in high school.

## College

- Initially enrolled as a mechanical engineering major, then switched to civil engineering.
- Earned a civil engineering degree from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

## Civil Engineering Career (2011–2016)

- Moved to Chicago, IL in 2011.
- Worked as a civil engineer / construction manager at T.Y. Lin International for five years.
- Worked on major transportation infrastructure projects, including the first high-speed rail line in Illinois (Springfield to Chicago).
- Eventually realized construction management was not his long-term path, which led him to discover coding.

## Career Change to Software Engineering (2016–present)

- Moved to New York City in 2016 to pursue software engineering — arrived with no job lined up and without having seen his apartment beforehand.
- Attended Flatiron School, a coding bootcamp, in 2016.
- Lived in multiple Brooklyn neighborhoods including Bushwick, Greenpoint, and Crown Heights.
- Landed his first software engineering role in 2017 at Giant Machines as the 14th employee.
- Initially focused on front-end development, then expanded into full-stack applications.
- Rose through the ranks to become a principal engineer.
- Giant Machines grew to over 100 employees and was eventually acquired by Deloitte.

## AI & Professional Interests

- Deeply interested in generative AI and applied AI, particularly as AI emerged as a major industry force during his time at Giant Machines / Deloitte.
- Aspires to work at Anthropic.
- Describes himself as a creator at heart who loves to build things.

## Running

- Avid and serious runner who trains heavily, sometimes logging 90–100 miles per week.
- Personal bests: 1:11:28 half marathon, 2:33:53 marathon.
